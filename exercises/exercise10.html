Hello there!

My name's Kalana Amarasekara, and I'm originally from Sri Lanka. I'm a junior English major and public policy minor taking the PWTC certificate here at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. As with most people, I decided to take the PWTC certificate because it had the best future job prospects, but I have also come to genuinely enjoy taking it.

I have learned to create easily accessible digital material that all audiences can use. When I'm not coding or presenting information, I write for the Massachusetts Daily Collegian, where I have served as an Assistant Arts Editor for almost two years (my profile can be found here), and I am also an intern in the Editorial, Design and Production department of the University of Massachusetts Press, the University's publishing house.

My interests include:

  • Journalism
  • Professional writing
  • User experience
  • Technical documentation
  • Policy writing

For getting rid of the white space below my footer, I Googled many solutions. Eventually, I found that giving the body a margin of 0 would stop the white space below the footer from spilling over, but the text would often spill beyond the margins. So, I created a Main selector in my CSS and applied a padding of 10px to that, so the text would still be legible and there would be no space below the footer.
