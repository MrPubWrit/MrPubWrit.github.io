Stakeholder Analysis

View the Stakeholder Analysis (PDF)

I wrote this stakeholder analysis for my introductory Public Policy class (SPP 280), analyzing the stakeholders involved in the implementation of the state of Massachusetts's Affordable Homes Act. This assignment taught me more about what goes into implementing public policy, especially when policies are tangible to people, and ensuring that it can be carried out. Analyzing what goes into policies requires writing, especially when discussing those policies with other people, so this assignment allowed me to employ various professional writing techniques.

Position Paper on Artificial Intelligence

View the Position Paper (PDF)

I wrote this essay for my course Defending Democracy in the Digital World (SPP 208), arguing that generative artificial intelligence could be mitigated as a threat to democracy when regulated by stakeholders. This assignment taught me about how to make defensible and rhetorically compelling arguments about public policy and the media, especially during the rise of artificial intelligence as a technology. Making solid logical arguments, a cornerstone of professional writing, requires conducting extensive research on topics and making that research widely readable.
